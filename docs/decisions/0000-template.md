## Context

What's the situation? What forces are at play? Be specific about the constraint or question that prompted this decision. Don't pad with preamble — get to the technical substance.

## Decision

What did we decide? Stated as an active choice, not a description.

## Consequences

What follows from this decision? Include both:

- **Positive:** what gets easier or better
- **Negative or accepted trade-offs:** what gets harder, what we give up, what risks we accept

Be honest about the negatives — an ADR with only positives is hiding something.

## Alternatives considered

What other options did we look at? For each, a one-paragraph summary of why it wasn't chosen. Future readers (including the people who made the decision a year ago) need this context to evaluate whether the decision still holds.
